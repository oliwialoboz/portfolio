Parafia pw. Ducha Świętego w Tychach

This website was designed during my student internship as part of a project to redesign an outdated website template. The goal was to create a modern, user-friendly, and visually appealing layout that reflects the spiritual and cultural values of the parish community.

Clients are presented with multiple template options, and this particular design was selected as a preferred choice. The final website is then tailored to their specific content and information, such as parish announcements, Mass intentions, liturgical schedules, and contact details.

The template features clear navigation, an elegant visual style, and dedicated sections for current news, podcasts, sacraments, and daily intentions.

Parafia Demonstracyjna w Katowicach

This is another example of a website I designed during my student internship. It was created as part of a library of ready-made templates from which clients can choose the one that best fits their needs. The goal was to deliver a modern, clear, and aesthetically pleasing layout that could be easily adapted to the unique character of each parish.

Once a client chooses this template, their website is customized with their own information, including announcements, liturgical calendars, Mass intentions, and community updates.

The design emphasizes intuitive navigation, a harmonious color scheme, and dedicated areas for parish news, devotional resources, and event highlights.

Human Resource Menagement

This project is another example of a website I designed during my student internship. The goal was to create a modern and professional template for an HR management system that simplifies business operations and employee workflows.

The layout highlights key system features such as time tracking, absence and leave management, work scheduling, and employee benefits. Clear pricing plans and a strong call-to-action guide users toward exploring the system.

The design emphasizes usability, clean visuals, and a business-oriented aesthetic suitable for corporate clients.

Demo Expo Support

This project is an example of refreshing and modernizing an existing website. The task was to create a new design for the online ticket shop and the participant zone, focusing on improved readability, user convenience, and overall aesthetics.

The redesigned shop introduces a clear presentation of tickets, transparent pricing, and an intuitive purchase process. The participant zone was restructured to guide users smoothly through registration, purchase, and payment steps.

The result is a modern, user-friendly interface that makes navigation simple and the entire process more efficient.
